Example Comic Page for Screen Reader

Panel 1: This is the first panel of the page.
Traditionally, comics are read top to bottom, left to right.

In the case of this comic, that's the rule we'll use.
Panel 2: For abnormal grids, or the way panels are arranged, the rule still applies.
A long vertical panel like this will be read next, since it's the leftmost panel, even if it technically takes up two rows.

Normally, smaller panels won't have this much text.
Panel 3: The next panel is to the right of the vertical panel, finishing the rest of this row.
All of these panels have the same gutters, which is the empty space between panels.
Panel 4: This page could be described as a 3 by 4 grid.
Panel 5: That's 3 panels across, and 4 panels tall.
