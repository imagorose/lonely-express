Characters

Via

The sole employee and owner of Mercurio Mail, an independent interspace mail delivery service. Height, age, and birthday are unknown in antiquated Earth standards, though a simple glance would tell you that she's above average height and about twenty five years alive.

Asa

A bubbly girl that would call herself Via's friend (unbeknownst to Via). It's unknown what she does for a living, but it's apparently lucrative enough for her to be at the late night diner Via frequents almost daily. She's 5'4", and 24 years old (and a Libra).
